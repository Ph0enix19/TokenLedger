# Acme Corp HR Policy

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9am-6pm Monday to Friday. Flexible working is available with manager approval.

## Leave Entitlement
All full-time employees receive 14 days annual leave per year, increasing to 18 days after 3 years.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. A minimum of 2 days in-office is required.

## Expense Claims
All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Claims above RM 500 require manager approval.
