# Acme Corp Expense Rules

## Approved Categories
- Travel: flights, hotels, ground transport for company business
- Meals: client entertainment up to RM 200 per person
- Equipment: home office setup up to RM 2,000 per year
- Training: courses and conferences up to RM 5,000 per year

## Submission Process
1. Submit via Expense Portal within 30 days
2. Attach receipts for items above RM 50
3. Manager approval for items above RM 500
4. Finance approval for items above RM 2,000

## Not Reimbursable
Personal items, fines, alcohol (unless client entertainment and manager approved), and items purchased for personal use are not reimbursable.
